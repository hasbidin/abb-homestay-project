Amenities and Facilities

Ababoo Homestay (SJ 28) is a spacious corner lot house located at Lorong Sepat Jaya 3. Fully equipped with air-conditioner for master bedroom, and fans for the rest - the rooms are breezy, bright, clean and comfortable. The whole house is fitted with tile flooring and large windows, allowing natural sunlight to brighten its atmosphere. The living room comes with a seating area and a TV. Kitchen is fully functional. Hot-water showers are included in both bathrooms. Iron and ironing board are provided as well.

Rooms

Four rooms consist of one master bedroom with air-conditioner, two rooms with two beds each, and one room with one bed.

Parking

Ample amount of space available for parking, including one covered under the porch.

Beach

Walking distance to the beach.

Space

Huge corner lot space at this cosy beachfront accommodation can arrange for activities including barbeque and any other event for all guests.

Kitchen

Available with fridge, kettle, rice cooker, utensils, coffee/tea and mineral water.
